# Operations, Attack Cutscenes, and Communications

## Interactive beginning

A new campaign opens with the fall of Crater Market beneath the orbiting Peacekeeper station. The player chooses how the Syndicate began:

- Rescue the survivors: repairs the Street Clinic and grants the highest Crew Trust, but creates more Heat.
- Strip the Authority wreckage: grants Lunar Alloy, Helium-3, and Credits.
- Steal station access codes: grants Authority Data Cores, Intel, and an early Black Tech level, but creates the most Heat.

The decision is saved with the criminal profile.

## Three harvesting resources

- Lunar Alloy: hideout armor, blast doors, and sentry construction.
- Helium-3: energy, ships, and research operations.
- Authority Data Cores: hacking, signal jamming, counter-hacking, and Black Tech.

Each site has a timer, yield, and Heat risk.

## Take Back threats

The shared MoonGoons Peacekeeper force attacks through four response types:

- Survey Drone sweeps
- Patrol Deputy capture teams
- Peacekeeper cyber warrants
- Riot Vanguard breaches

Each attack opens an interactive cutscene with Fight, Hide, and Counter-Hack choices. Failed Patrol Deputy responses can capture a crew member. Captured crew unlock prisoner-transfer rescue puzzles.

## Hideout defenses

- Signal Jammer Grid
- Sentry Turret Network
- Armored Blast Doors
- Emergency Escape Tunnels

All four are upgradeable with the three harvesting resources.

## Side missions

- Hidden-object evidence-room theft
- Peacekeeper law-network hacking
- Rival Syndicate cipher cracking
- Captured-crew rescue hacking

## Communications

The in-game communications screen includes:

- Galaxy chat
- Alliance chat
- Private chat with a recipient field
- Real text entry
- Saved local channel history

The current transport is explicitly labeled Local Prototype. Messages work and persist on the same device. Cross-device player delivery requires a secure chat backend; the client state is structured so that transport can be added without replacing the interface.

## Navigation

The rotating lunar hideout bottom navigation now links to Hideout, Scores, Operations, Chat, and Save.
