# Full Isometric Art Pass

This branch replaces the temporary diagram-like world with a complete original 2.5D lunar base-builder art package inspired by the approved camera angle and presentation.

## Art atlases

- `buildings_atlas`: twelve distinct cutaway hideout buildings with workers and equipment.
- `systems_atlas`: three resource sites, four defenses, and four Peacekeeper threats.
- `npc_atlas`: eight animated job roles with four movement/work frames each.
- `vehicles_atlas`: Syndicate rover, smuggler skiff, Peacekeeper shuttle, and orbital station.
- `ui_atlas`: Patrol, Heroes, DermaPack, Store, Alliance, State, Rotate, and Zoom icons.
- `board_terrain`: lunar roads, pads, fences, craters, props, lights, and a moon horizon.
- `cutscene_atlas`: origin, Ghost Key, district war-room, and finale scenes using the same world art.
- `attack_atlas`: Survey Drone, Patrol Deputy, Riot Vanguard, and cyber-warrant attack scenes.

## Playable presentation

Buildings remain tied to repair, upgrade, research, training, healing, market, and smuggling systems. NPCs follow visible job routes and use role-specific animation frames. Vehicles move along the lunar roads. Attack and story scenes use the same art vocabulary as the playable board. Camera drag, zoom, center, and quarter-turn rotation remain available.
