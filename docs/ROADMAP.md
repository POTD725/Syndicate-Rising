# Production roadmap

## Phase 1: Standalone foundation

- direct portrait launch
- Syndicate city
- eight buildings
- crew roster
- criminal score board
- tactical raids
- story cinematics
- save/load
- Web and Android exports

## Phase 2: APK-style city depth

- animated workers and crew
- construction slots
- building interiors
- camera pan and zoom
- contextual building menus
- resource collection bubbles
- mission notifications

## Phase 3: Progression

- hero equipment and skills
- troop training
- healing and recovery
- research tree
- daily and chapter missions
- VIP and achievement systems using original MoonGoons design

## Phase 4: Lunar underworld map

- territories
- resource sites
- rival syndicates
- marches and rallies
- alliance territory
- boss encounters

## Phase 5: Social and live systems

- alliances
- mail
- chat hooks
- stores
- events
- rankings
- seasonal content
