# Isometric Lunar Board and DermaPack

## Canonical board presentation

The Syndicate Rising city is presented as a polished three-quarter isometric lunar settlement rather than a straight-down map or diagrammatic room grid.

The board contains:

- A visible Peacekeeper orbital station above the moon.
- Individual criminal buildings with readable interiors and exterior machinery.
- Workers, vehicles, patrol craft, roads, landing pads, lights, and defensive activity.
- Twelve interactive room-system hotspots tied to the existing repair, upgrade, research, training, healing, market, and smuggling logic.
- Camera drag, zoom, recentering, and 90-degree rotation controls.

The board art is embedded in the Godot project so browser and Android exports do not depend on an external image host.

## DermaPack

The former backpack concept is replaced by the **DermaPack**, a wearable micro-storage system with:

- Biometric and dermal-link security.
- Compact mission loadout storage.
- Contraband, Intel, Data Core, Lunar Alloy, Helium-3, and Black Tech readouts.
- A dedicated wearable-device icon and bottom-navigation entry.

## Mobile navigation

The bottom bar follows the intended mobile base-builder language:

- Patrol
- Heroes
- DermaPack
- Store
- Alliance
- State

Operations and profile saving remain available as side quick controls.
