# MoonGoons: Syndicate Rising — Lunar Hideout Canon

## Shared-world setting

MoonGoons: Syndicate Rising takes place on and beneath the surface of the same moon used by the Peacekeeper-side game. The Syndicate operates from a damaged underground criminal hideout while the Peacekeeper police space station orbits overhead.

The station is a living threat, not background decoration. Heat controls the intensity of orbital surveillance:

- Low Heat: routine patrol watch.
- Medium Heat: active scanning cones sweep the lunar surface.
- High Heat: raid warnings and Peacekeeper response pressure.

A visible power core feeds the connected room network. The moon surface above includes concealed outposts, antennas, craters, and access points.

## Game connection

Syndicate Rising and the Peacekeeper-side game describe opposite sides of the same conflict:

- Criminal research reverse-engineers Peacekeeper technology.
- Hacker training poisons orbital surveillance.
- High Heat causes stronger station pressure.
- Smuggler traffic and lunar jobs create incidents the Peacekeepers can later investigate.
- Future boarding missions will allow the Syndicate to attack or infiltrate the orbiting station.

## Visual direction

The city card grid is retired. The canonical opening view is the living lunar hideout cutaway with the orbiting station visible at all times.
